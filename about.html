About BackboneJobs

India's First Dedicated Job Portal for Support Staff - Because Every Job Matters

Our Story

BackboneJobs was born from a simple observation: While countless job portals exist for corporate and IT professionals, the hardworking support staff who form the backbone of every organization were being overlooked. Security guards ensuring our safety, housekeeping staff maintaining our spaces, drivers keeping us mobile, and facility managers ensuring smooth operations - they all deserved a dedicated platform.

Founded in 2024, we set out to bridge this critical gap in India's job market. We recognized that these professionals face unique challenges: limited digital literacy, language barriers, and lack of access to quality job opportunities. Traditional job portals weren't designed with them in mind.

Today, BackboneJobs is transforming how support staff find employment and how employers discover talent. We've simplified the application process, enabled direct communication via WhatsApp, and created a platform that respects the dignity and importance of every job role. Our mission is to ensure that every support staff professional in India has access to fair, transparent, and quality employment opportunities.
